# Privacy Policy for the “Gradle” Chrome Extension

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens locally in the browser.

The Gradle Chrome extension is designed to provide a fun, Wordle-style guessing game for users viewing their own assessment scores on Crowdmark.
This extension operates entirely on the user’s local device.

## Information Collection and Use

Gradle does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.
All processing occurs locally in your browser. The extension never sends information to any external server or service.

Specifically:
- No personal information is collected.
- No browsing data is collected.
- No score or grade information is transmitted outside the device.
- No analytics, tracking, or logging is performed.

## Permissions:
The extension only runs on pages that match:

`https://app.crowdmark.com/*`

This is required solely to replace elements of the page with the grade-guessing game.
The extension cannot access any other websites or data.

## Third-Party Services
Gradle does not use any third-party services, APIs, analytics platforms, or storage.

## Data Storage
No data is stored.
Everything disappears as soon as the page is closed or refreshed.

## Children’s Privacy
The extension is safe for all users. No information is collected from any users, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
If this extension’s functionality changes, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
Users should review this page periodically for updates.
